v0.27 Enterprise Evidence Export

Proof, ledger, replay, graph, coverage, and audit artifacts are exported as a hash-verified evidence bundle.

Goal

Give enterprise security teams a portable bundle that can be stored, verified, and reviewed without trusting a generated summary alone.

Bundle Contents

manifest.json with hashes
ledger.jsonl as fact source
proof.json as portable summary
replay.html
path graph JSON/HTML
path-policy.json
coverage.html
audit JSON/HTML

Audit Questions

The audit report is structured around who, what, why, policy, approval, outcome, coverage, and gate status.

CLI

kappaski evidence export --ledger ledger.jsonl --out-dir .kappaski/evidence
kappaski evidence verify --bundle .kappaski/evidence/manifest.json
kappaski audit report --ledger ledger.jsonl --out-dir .kappaski/audit
