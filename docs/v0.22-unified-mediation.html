v0.22 Unified Mediation Contract

One request, decision, and outcome contract across wrapper, native hook, MCP, shim, and daemon surfaces.

Contract

MediationRequest->MediationDecision->Approval / Enforcement->MediationOutcome->Ledger

Effects

allow, audit, require_approval, deny, enforced_block, and fail_open_alert are normalized before proof and replay consume them.

CLI

kappaski mediation inspect --ledger ledger.jsonl
kappaski mediation resolve --ledger ledger.jsonl --mediation med_abc --actor security --status approved --reason expected
kappaski mediation replay --ledger ledger.jsonl
